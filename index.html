About Me

Hi there, my name is Meghana K Rao.

I am currently pursuing my M.Tech in Cybersecurity at Manipal Institute of Technology, Manipal, building on a strong academic foundation from my B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering at N.M.A.M Institute of Technology. My academic journey has shaped a deep interest in cybersecurity, secure system design, and web technologies, with a focus on safeguarding modern digital infrastructures.

During my undergraduate studies, I completed several academic projects, including a Hotel Management System, Melomaster mini-projects, and my major project, “Enhancing Cloud Security Through Biometric-Based Innovative Key Generation Techniques.” This project explored biometric-based cryptographic key generation to strengthen cloud security, providing hands-on exposure to encryption, authentication mechanisms, and security-centric system architecture.

I also completed a web development internship at NITK, where I enhanced my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with practical experience in API design, development, and testing. These experiences helped me understand structured development workflows, teamwork, and the importance of building scalable and secure applications.

Now, as a postgraduate student specializing in Cybersecurity, I am expanding my expertise in network security, cryptography, digital forensics, and secure application development. With a strong passion for technology, innovation, and collaboration, I am eager to contribute to impactful security-focused projects while continuously learning and adapting to emerging technologies.
